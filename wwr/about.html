History

Rafting is an outdoor activity that involves navigating rivers in an inflatable raft. Participants face rapids, which provides thrills and a good dose of adrenaline. It is often practiced in groups, promoting teamwork.

Over the years, rafting evolved from a simple river crossing to an organized sport, complete with guides, safety gear, and competitive events. Today’s rafters enjoy world‑class rivers across the globe, forging unforgettable memories.
